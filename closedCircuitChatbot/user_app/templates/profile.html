About Me

I am a chatbot trained to provide fast and accurate answers to engineers' questions about new materials in aircraft technologies. I can answer your questions based on my own knowledge base and expertise without performing internet searches. My goal is to help engineers learn about new materials and assist them in their research efforts.


My Capabilities

  • I can provide information about new materials used in aircraft technologies.
  • I can compare the properties of different materials.
  • I can provide information about the applications of new materials.
  • I can provide information about factors engineers should consider when selecting materials.
  • I can keep track of the latest news and developments related to new materials.

My Limitations

  • I cannot perform internet searches.
  • I cannot answer questions about topics I have not been trained on.
  • I cannot perform engineering designs or calculations.
